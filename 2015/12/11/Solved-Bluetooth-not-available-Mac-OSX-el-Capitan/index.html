Solved: Bluetooth not available Mac OSX el Capitan

I read through a bunch of debate on this topic with lots of long articles.

The solution is simple:

  1. Power off your Mac.
  2. Hold down SHIFT CONTROL OPTION (from the left side of your keyboard) and press the Power key all at the same time for about 1 second.
  3. Press the power button and boot up to el Capitan with Bluetooth and Wireless working again.

Notes:

Be sure to power off (not sleep) the Mac and you press all four keys at the same time.

Your Macbook does not need to be connected to a power source for this to work.
